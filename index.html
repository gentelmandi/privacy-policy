Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

👋 Introduction

Welcome to Mindset Shift ("we", "our", "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your information. The short version: we don't collect any personal data.

Mindset Shift is a fully offline application. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with third parties.

📱 Information We Do NOT Collect

Mindset Shift does not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Health or biometric data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, contacts, or other device content

💾 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device to provide its core functionality:

  • User Preferences — Your selected topics of interest, notification schedule settings, and theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Coaching Messages History — A local database of coaching messages that have been delivered to you, including favorites you have saved
  • Onboarding Status — Whether you have completed the initial setup process

This data never leaves your device. It is not synchronized to any cloud service, server, or third-party platform.

🔔 Notifications

Mindset Shift uses local notifications to deliver coaching messages, motivational quotes, and personalized reminders throughout your day. These notifications are:

  • Scheduled and delivered entirely on your device
  • Not powered by any external push notification service
  • Fully configurable — you control the frequency and active hours
  • Revocable at any time through your device's Settings

🔒 Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption, passcode protection, and biometric authentication (if enabled).

If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted from your device.

🌐 Third-Party Services

Mindset Shift does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not make network requests or connect to external servers during normal operation.

👶 Children's Privacy

Mindset Shift does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The App does not collect personal information from any user, regardless of age.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
